Genetic algo for swarm dynamics
Each drone emits weak signal (how weak = gene) that decays according to inv sq law
Use this signal to determine distance and role of drone
Drones may change role according to some prob function
Drones have additional signal for which "clan" they're with if they want to make sub-swarms

Each drone knows:
	last transmitted location of target
	strength/type of signal of nearest neighbors (assume a 3d vector)
	its own role
	its own velocity vector
	its current clan
	distance above ground
	relative location to stable point

Objectives:
	don't collide with neighbors
	don't collide with ground
	destroy (possibly moving) target(s)
	do so as quickly as possible

Actions:
	Fire weapon (optional; limited capacity)
	Change role (optional)
	Accelerate in direction (optional)
	Message pass (passive)
		current location
		current role
		location of target (if visible)
		velocity vector
